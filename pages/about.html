About Us

Learn about Narasimha Group and our commitment to serving the community.

Welcome to Narasimha Group

Narasimha Group is a trusted name in Ankola, Karnataka, offering diverse services that cater to the everyday needs of our community. With years of dedicated service, we have established ourselves as a one-stop solution for groceries, financial services, branded supplies, and retail stores.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide quality products and services at competitive prices while maintaining the highest standards of customer satisfaction. We believe in building lasting relationships with our customers through trust, reliability, and excellence.

Our Services

Narasimha Group comprises four main business verticals:

  • Narasimha Big Bazar: A modern retail store with over 11,000+ products including groceries, personal care, household items, and more.
  • Nidhi Souharda Co-Operative Society: Trusted financial services offering savings, deposits, and loan services with attractive interest rates.
  • Narasimha Suppliers: A reliable wholesale and retail distributor serving quality groceries and Ayurvedic products for over 9 years.
  • Narasimha Stores: Centrally located in Ankola city, offering convenient access to daily essentials.

Our Values

  • Trust: We build relationships based on honesty and transparency.
  • Quality: We never compromise on the quality of our products and services.
  • Customer First: Our customers are at the heart of everything we do.
  • Community: We are committed to serving and supporting our local community.
